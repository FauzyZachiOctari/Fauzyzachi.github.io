Sistem Informasi Peraturan Perusahaan Dinas Tenaga Kerja Kota Pekanbaru

This information system is designed and built to be simple and user-friendly, aiming to facilitate employees in using the system effectively. The system serves the purpose of data management, specifically for documents like Bipartite Work Agreement (LKS), Company Regulations (PP), and Collective Work Agreement (PKB) between the company and the Pekanbaru City Manpower Office. This data management system streamlines employee bookkeeping and document submission by storing the required files into the database of the Pekanbaru City Manpower Office.

Sistem Pakar Diagnosa Stres Siswa SMK N 1 Pasir Penyu saat Pembelajaran Daring

This expert system is designed and built to diagnose the stress level of students at Vocational Highschool 1 Pasir Penyu during the Covid-19 pandemic. The system can determine the diagnosis of a student's stress level, such as no stress, mild stress, moderate stress, and severe stress, by providing answers to questions provided by the teacher within this expert system. The system will record students' responses and display the diagnosis along with solutions that the students can follow to reduce their stress levels.

Sistem Pakar Diagnosa Penyakit Ikan Nila

This expert system is designed and built to facilitate fish farmers (specifically for tilapia fish/Ikan Nila) in diagnosing diseases that affect tilapia fish by answering questions provided by freshwater fish experts. In this system, user responses will be recorded and calculated using the certainty factor method. The system will provide results in the form of a diagnosis of the tilapia fish disease, suggested solutions, information about the diagnosed disease, and preventive measures. This expert system aims to assist fish farmers in making accurate and informed decisions to manage and prevent diseases that may impact their tilapia fish stocks.
